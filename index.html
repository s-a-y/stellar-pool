FAQ

How it works?

Stellar has Inflation (1% per annum), which distributed weekly between 50 most voted accounts (or less if any of them passes 1.5% of total STR number).

We join together and vote for the same account to get the credit. If our pool is among the winners of Inflation credit - reward will be distributed between pool members proportionally to their voting power.

Voting power is your wallet balance at the moment of Inflation distribution.

How to join

To join simply login to official Stellar client, go to "Settings" by clicking your username in top-right corner. Then choose "change inflation destination". Put pool address 
gaj43wzt5XD8fz6JfbnaVQsewLtdfU5cFK
into the field and press "set".

Does pool already receive rewards?

Not yet, at the moment we're too small. We need to reach goal of 1.5bln STR voting power to start getting rewards.

How much do I get?

Once the pool is big enough - every member will receive rewards weekly, proportionally to their wallet balance at the moment of Inflation. Minimum reward is 1% per year or 0.02% per week. Reward may be 100x bigger, if other members of STR network are not organized.
